The Curtin Robotics Club (CRoC) is a club for people who love to create and build electronic projects. We build robots, participate in robotics competitions, teach high school students to build a robot and much more. Our objective is to create a fun environment for students who share the same interest.

If you would like to join, or would like to know more, there are many ways you can contact us.
